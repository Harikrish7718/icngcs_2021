About ICNGCS 2021

The advancements in computing have led to the development of intelligent systems that are data-driven, sense-making and insight-extraction. These systems emulate the human cognitive process and could respond to the fast-changing data and conditions in real-time. The Internet of Things, connected “smart” devices, that interact with each other through the internet generate voluminous, heterogeneous data. These structured and unstructured data from these devices if analysed can provide personalised insights at the right time in the right context and with evidence. The technology evolutions in different domains necessitate a detailed discussion on the research opportunities in these fields.


PSG Institute Of Technology and Applied Research

PSG Institute of Technology and Applied Research established in the year 2014, as the 30th Institution under the umbrella of PSG Sons’ and charities, is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. PSG iTech has a great vision to achieve academic excellence and to nurture the future engineers with required skills to face the global challenges. This institute offers five Under Graduate courses Civil, CSE, ECE, EEE and Mechanical Engineering. The institute has excellent infrastructure facilities, well-equipped laboratories with state-of-art equipments and Centers of Excellence (CoE) for intensive training and for research & consultancy activities. To promote industry interaction and to make the students industry the institute has signed MoU with various research organizations and industries across the globe. The Institute envision at producing engineers with strong interpersonal skills and societal consciousness.




Computer Science and Engineering Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering has a vision of producing the technocrats with core-technical competency. To keep abreast with the latest technological developments like SMAC technologies (Social network analysis, Mobile computing, Analytics and Cloud computing) the students are trained in the latest hardware and software technologies. Their analytical, computational thinking, problem solving skills of the students are kindled by the well-qualified dedicated faculty members of the department. The salient features of the department include placements in leading IT and ITES companies, regular invited lectures and workshops on advanced topics and inclusion of value-added courses to supplement their curriculum.

Guidelines to authors for paper submission

  • General instructions to be followed

    Prospective authors are required to submit full-length papers of 6 pages maximum in IEEE format. Author(s)/Co-author(s) can submit maximum two papers.

  • Regarding plagairism

    Submitted research work must be original in content. Plagiarism found at any stage of publication will lead to rejection of paper from conference. Authors are required to use iThenticate/Turnitin for checking plagiarism and it must be below 20% to qualify paper to be reviewed. ICNGCS 2021 doesn't support even self plagiarism. Authors submitting the review type of work must be very cautious about the same.

  • Publication

    The proceedings will be published with International Standard Book Number (ISBN: 978-93-89515-35-0).

  • Submission

    Submission of Manuscripts can be done through given registration link.
    Please note the following while submitting manuscript by Email:
    • Quote the subject line as : ICNGCS 2021 Paper Submission : "Paper Title"
    • Manuscript to be sent in .pdf format only.
